Atom Structure

Every atom has a nucleus with electrons flying around it like satellites. The nucleus contains protons and neutrons.

The opposite electric charges of the protons and electrons hold the electrons close to the nucleus.

Notice the electrons moving around the nucleus. Much like the moon orbits the earth, electrons orbit the atom's nucleus.
